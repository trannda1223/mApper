About the Game

Parachute was born out of a love of travel and a desire to share the world with our friends.

We wanted to build an easy-to-use, shareable platform that would allow users to not only participate in the game, but expand and shape the game as they played. Any location a user shares with their friends is stored in our database, so the difficulty and variety of locations increases as more people add their favorite spots.

We love feedback and collaboration! If you've spotted a bug, or would like to contribute, fork our repo on github
